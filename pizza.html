Pizza Restaurant

You’ve always loved pizza, so you decide to open a pizza restaurant.

You spend hours making mood boards on Pinterest, asking your neighbor Danny how to make the best pizza, and hiring employees from Indeed.

Finally, your business is ready to open!

You have everything: A bar with no room for a bartender, pizzas that barely fit on the tables, booths that have no seats, indoor plants, and a color scheme that was originally intended to make people think it’s a Domino's, but doesn’t trick anyone.

Now, you have to decide if you want to focus on profit to make a quick buck (but your business may go under, and you may anger a pyromaniac), or focus on quality (and build a loyal following).

BANK BALANCE: $150,000
